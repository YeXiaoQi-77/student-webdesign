GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is a long-term increase in Earth's average temperature. It is primarily driven by human activities.

Learn More

CAUSES

The main cause is the emission of greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide and methane.

EFFECTS

The effects include rising sea levels, more frequent heatwaves, and shifting weather patterns.

SOLUTIONS

Mitigating global warming requires reducing greenhouse gas emissions and adopting sustainable practices.
